What's New?

.

June 8, 2021 Release

The June 8, 2021 release focused on enhancements, improvements, and resolving bugs.

Enhancements

The Important flag was added to Tasks, Issues, and Risks

From the details page of a task, issue, or risk, the user can identify the item as important by selecting the Important flag.

When marked as important the yellow star will be visible:

otherwise it will be gray:

You can toggle between being marked Important and not marked important by simply selecting the word Important or the star.

To save space in Sheet views, Kanban cards, and Map cards only the yellow star will be shown.

The On Hold flag was added to Tasks, Issues, and Risks

From the details page of an item, the user can put the item on hold by selecting the On Hold flag.

When marked as on hold the blue pause circle will be visible:

Otherwise it will be gray:

You can toggle between being On Hold and not on hold by simply selecting the words On Hold and/or the blue pause circle.

In most areas of the system, to save space only the blue pause circle will be shown to represent an item as being On Hold.

Items on hold will not be included in any metrics or counts and can only be seen with the proper filter being set. To be able to see on hold items, the On Hold filter, and only the On Hold filter should be selected.

The Draft flag was added to Tasks, Issues, and Risks

From the details page of an item, the user can mark the item as a draft by selecting the Draft flag.

When marked as a draft the yellow pencil will be visible:

Otherwise it will be gray:

You can toggle between being a Draft and not a draft by simply selecting the word Draft and/or the yellow pencil.

In most areas of the system, to save space only the yellow pencil will be shown to represent an item as being a Draft.

Items that are drafts will not be included in any metrics or counts and can only be seen with the proper filter being set. To be able to see draft items, the Drafts filter, and only the Drafts filter should be selected.

The Ongoing flag was added to Tasks and Risks

From the details page of a task or risk, the user can mark the item as ongoing by selecting the Ongoing flag. Issues cannot be marked as ongoing.

When marked as ongoing the green cyclical box will be visible:

Otherwise it will be gray:

You can toggle between being Ongoing and not ongoing by simply selecting the word Ongoing and/or the green cyclical box.

In most areas of the system, to save space only the green cyclical box will be shown to represent an item as being a Ongoing. Tasks and risks that are marked as ongoing have some different attributes:

  • They cannot be overdue as there is no Start Date or Due Date

  • There is a not required Date Identified and they can be closed by setting the Date Closed

  • They have no progress or percentage complete, so they are excluded from those summary and overview calculations

  • There are separate metrics for how many tasks and risks are ongoing

The Disposition tab was enabled for Risks

To be able to capture what is the future of a risk, the Disposition tab was enabled.

Calendar view was implemented

To be able to see tasks, issues, and risks from a calendar perspective, a calendar view was implemented.

The vertical double green bars shows the start date or date identified and the vertical double red bars shows the due date or date closed.

Double clicking on the either of those will open the details page of the item. A left click on either of those items will display a pop-up with information about the item as well as the ability to delete the item or access its details pages.
